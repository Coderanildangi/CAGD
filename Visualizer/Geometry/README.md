## Introduction

The Geometry DLL encapsulates essential functionalities for dealing with geometric entities:

- **Points:** Representation and manipulation of points in a 2D or 3D space.
- **Lines:** Operations related to lines, including intersection, length calculation, etc.
- **Shapes:** Fundamental geometric shapes like circles, rectangles, polygons, etc.

The DLL aims to provide an efficient and modular solution for geometry-related operations in various applications.

## Features

- **Point Operations:** Creation, manipulation, and calculations involving points in 2D/3D space.
- **Line Functions:** Intersection, length calculation, and other operations associated with lines.
- **Shape Handling:** Creation, properties, and operations for various geometric shapes.
